Topic 3: Digital Etiquette

What it is

Digital Etiquette, or netiquette, refers to the appropriate and respectful way of communicating and behaving in the digital world.

Importance

Practicing good digital manners ensures positive interactions and helps build a respectful online community.

Challenges

  • Misunderstandings caused by lack of tone or context
  • Online arguments and trolling
  • Unintended offense due to cultural differences

Tips & Strategies

  • Always think before posting or commenting
  • Use respectful and inclusive language
  • Observe community rules and guidelines
  • Don't engage in online arguments
  • Respect others' opinions even if you disagree

Practicing digital etiquette not only reflects your character but also promotes a healthier online space for all.
