Topic 2: Online Privacy

Overview

Online privacy is about safeguarding your personal information in the digital world. In an age where almost everything is connected, protecting your data has become a crucial skill.

Common Challenges

  • Unintentional oversharing on social media
  • Weak or reused passwords
  • Falling for phishing emails or scams

Tips & Strategies

  • Use unique, complex passwords for different accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible
  • Think twice before clicking links in emails or messages
  • Adjust your privacy settings on social platforms
  • Be aware of app permissions
