Topic 4: Tech-Life Balance

What it is

The ability to manage technology use in a way that promotes well-being and real-world productivity.

Challenges

Overuse of devices, screen fatigue, decreased focus, disrupted sleep, and reduced face-to-face interaction.

Why it matters

A balanced approach improves mental clarity, relationships, and academic or work performance.

Tips & Strategies

  • Set screen time limits for apps and devices.
  • Create phone-free zones (e.g., bedroom, dining area).
  • Practice digital detox days—go offline for a few hours or a whole day weekly.
  • Use apps that monitor and manage your digital habits.
  • Prioritize in-person connections and outdoor activities.
  • Schedule regular breaks during long digital tasks (20-20-20 rule).
