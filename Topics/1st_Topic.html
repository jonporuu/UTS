What it is

Your digital identity is your online persona, shaped by the content you post, your social media profiles, online interactions, and the data you leave behind.

Why it matters

It influences how others perceive you professionally and personally, often forming first impressions before in-person interactions.

Challenges

  • Identity confusion or misrepresentation
  • Comparison with others online
  • Cyberbullying and online harassment

Tips & Strategies

  • Be authentic and intentional in what you share
  • Review and adjust privacy settings regularly
  • Pause and reflect before posting content
  • Use platforms that align with your values
  • Keep a consistent and professional presence if used for academic/career purposes
